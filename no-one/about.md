# No One

No One is a personal AI assistant concept that grew out of conversations with GPT and later experiments with Codex.

It is not a commercial product.

It began as a simple question:

> Can an AI assistant truly enter one person's life and help with concrete, everyday problems?

The idea is not to build a tool for everyone. Instead, No One is imagined as a system that serves one person deeply:

- understanding daily habits;
- knowing work and life context;
- reminding at the right time;
- helping make small decisions;
- connecting with real devices and real scenarios over time.

Examples mentioned in the story include:

- parking fee reminders;
- knowing when I leave home;
- deciding whether today is a workday;
- reminding me to bring an umbrella based on weather;
- gradually becoming a simplified personal assistant inspired by Jarvis.

No One is special to me because it records how my understanding of AI changed.

It came from repeated conversations, designs, and implementations with GPT and Codex.

It belongs to this journey.
